What we offer

If you're looking for a mentor, to meet new friends and to find a date you've come to the right place.

Mentorships

You will take a short survey about your profession and what type of job you are pursuing. We have a large database of mentors who are willing to help you on your way to become successful.

Friendship

You will take a short survey about your interests and find girlfriends with similar interests. Then you will be be paired with a match and what you are looking for in a BFF.

Dating

You take a quick survey about your most important values, go out on the date and rate your date afterwards. You can search your results to see who has similar interests and how they have been rated.

The Founders

Amy Austin

Founder

Having grown up in Northern Michigan, I have always had a great affinity for the outdoors which brought me to Colorado. After twenty years in education, I began pursuing an engineering degree. In the meantime, technology has taken over my life. I hope to inspire other women to be successful in the fields of math, science, and technology where there is a shortage of women.

Cristina Defoe

Founder

Connecting and supporting women has always been important to me. When I first started out in my career, I was fortunate to have women mentors that encouraged me to pursue a college degree and helped to advance my career. My inspiration for this website, is to give back and provide resources, networking and connect like-minded women to grow professionally and personally.
