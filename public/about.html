What we offer

If you're looking for a mentor, to meet new friends and to find a date you've come to the right place.

Mentorships

You will take a short survey about your profession and what type of job you are pursuing. We have a database of mentors who are willing to help you on your way to become successful and be paired with a match.

Friendship

You will take a short survey about what you like and find friends with similar interests. Then you will be paired with a match.

Dating

You take a quick survey about your most important values, go out on the date and rate your date afterward. You can search your results to see who has similar interests and how they have been rated.

The Founders

Amy Austin

Having grown up in Northern Michigan, I have always had a great affinity for the outdoors which brought me to Colorado. After twenty years in education, I began pursuing an engineering degree. In the meantime, technology has taken over my life. I hope to inspire other women to be successful in the fields of math, science, and technology where there is a shortage of women.

Cristina Defoe

Connecting and supporting women has always been important to me. My inspiration for creating this website is to provide resources, networking, and connect women with similar interests.

Nick Tsoukas

I am seeking full-stack certification from the University of Denver and will graduate in July. Post graduation I am seeking to help train others in the area of web development. I hope to see all students, especially women, become developers which is why I truly support the mission behind WOCO.
